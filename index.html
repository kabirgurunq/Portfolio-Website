Who I Am

A Little About Me

My name is Kabir Gurung — a passionate filmmaker and visual storyteller based in Nepal. I'm drawn to the language of cinema: light, shadow, movement, and silence. Every frame is a chance to say something that words alone cannot.

Alongside filmmaking, I'm on a journey into the world of information technology, learning the craft of building things on the web and shaping digital experiences. I believe creativity and technology are two sides of the same coin.
